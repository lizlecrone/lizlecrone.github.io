FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Why the Malaria Consortium?

They are currently rated as GiveWell's top charity. Their impact per dollar is among the highest available, with an average cost of $4,500 per life saved. The evidence of their impact is exceptionally strong. See more here: https://www.givewell.org/charities/top-charities

How do I donate?

You can do it from this page: https://www.malariaconsortium.org/donate-to-malaria-consortium

How will you know about my donation?

Tell us at the wedding! We plan to have a place where everyone can write down their donations. They will email you a receipt so you can remember, even if you check all the boxes to prevent emails.
